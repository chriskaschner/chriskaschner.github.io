**What it took:**
- Designed the PCB in EAGLE, wrote the hardware specification, managed BOM cross-references for manufacturer component inventory
- Ran a successful Kickstarter campaign with backers worldwide
- Coordinated with PCB manufacturer through production — spec, prototype, manufacturing, fulfillment

Full product lifecycle: idea, design, crowdfunding, manufacturing, and shipping to customers.
